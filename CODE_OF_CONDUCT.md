# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Contributors are expected to keep communication technical, respectful, and focused on the work. Personal attacks, harassment, discrimination, and deliberately disruptive behavior are not acceptable.

## Expected behavior

- discuss code and evidence directly
- assume good intent, but challenge weak technical claims with specifics
- keep review comments actionable
- respect privacy and avoid sharing sensitive data from prompts or datasets

## Unacceptable behavior

- abusive or insulting language
- discrimination or harassment
- publishing private or sensitive project data without permission
- intentionally misleading claims about results, metrics, or reproducibility

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation for behavior that violates this policy.
